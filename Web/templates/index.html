About Us

We're a group of Computer Science Undergraduates looking to help others find a study group made for success. Through studying together for the Foundation Exam in the past, we were able to recognize the impact that having a group can have on productivity. For a lot of people it is very difficult to go out and form a group, especially us CS majors (lol), so let StudyBuddy do it for you.
